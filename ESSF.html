Englewood Social Service Finder


Objective: Help children in the Greater Englewood Area of Chicago find Social Services Near them.

Worked on context-aware visual-analysis of Englewood social services. An open source, mobile friendly, JavaScript web application for helping youth in the Greater Englewood area of Chicago find social services near them. Worked in a team of three Undergraduates to deploy the final product.

Languages: JavaScript, HTML, CSS, D3, Leaflet
